================================================================================
SHANGO POLY - BACKTESTING SIMULATION SYSTEM
================================================================================

WHAT WE BUILT:
A comprehensive 90-180 day backtesting simulation system that provides
an HONEST assessment of the arbitrage bot's profitability and viability.

================================================================================
KEY COMPONENTS:
================================================================================

1. BacktestingEngine (src/simulation/BacktestingEngine.js)
   - Simulates trading day-by-day over 90-180 days
   - Models realistic costs: gas, slippage, MEV competition
   - Tracks daily/weekly/monthly profitability
   - Calculates comprehensive risk metrics

2. MarketDataProvider (src/simulation/MarketDataProvider.js)
   - Simulates 6 market phases: bull, bear, consolidation, crisis, recovery, low
   - Realistic volatility, liquidity, and competition modeling
   - Gas price variation based on network conditions

3. ReportGenerator (src/simulation/ReportGenerator.js)
   - Displays comprehensive results
   - Provides HONEST verdict based on metrics
   - Exports JSON and CSV for analysis
   - Shows both why system could succeed AND fail

4. Simulation Runner (scripts/run-simulation.js)
   - Easy-to-use CLI tool
   - Configurable parameters
   - Progress tracking
   - Results visualization
